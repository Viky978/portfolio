# Ecommerce Data Strategy Portfolio

## Case Study 1: Global Apparel Scale-Up
**Snapshot:** 5.2x ROAS | +65% Purchases | +32% Avg Order Value
**Problem:** Stagnant growth and declining return on ad spend (ROAS) in competitive international markets.
**Strategy:**
- Developed a custom LTV (Lifetime Value) prediction model to guide acquisition bidding.
- Executed rigorous A/B testing on checkout flows and product recommendation engines.
- Refined audience segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis.
- Integrated offline conversion data to improve platform algorithm training.
**Impact:** Scaled monthly revenue by 3x while maintaining a ROAS above 5.0.

## Case Study 2: Electronics Retailer Efficiency
**Snapshot:** 4.1x ROAS | +48% Purchases | -12% CAC
**Problem:** High customer acquisition cost (CAC) and poor retention in the high-churn electronics sector.
**Strategy:**
- Implemented automated churn prediction and personalized win-back campaigns.
- Optimized search engine marketing (SEM) for high-margin product categories.
- Enhanced data tracking for cross-device user journeys.
**Impact:** Increased repeat purchase rate by 22% and reduced overall CAC by 12%.

# Property Digital Strategy Portfolio

## Case Study 1: High-Yield Residential Acquisition
**Snapshot:** +42% Bookings | -28% CPL | 14.2% Effective Rate
**Problem:** High cost-per-lead and low conversion from digital channels for a premium residential development.
**Strategy:**
- Implemented multi-touch attribution to identify high-value touchpoints.
- Segmented audiences based on intent-driven search behavior and high-net-worth demographics.
- Optimized landing page funnels for lead quality rather than quantity.
- Automated lead scoring to prioritize sales team follow-ups.
**Impact:** Reduced overall marketing spend by 15% while increasing qualified bookings by 42%.

## Case Study 2: Commercial Portfolio Optimization
**Snapshot:** +35% Sales | -18% CPL | 9.5% Effective Rate
**Problem:** Fragmented data across multiple commercial properties leading to inefficient budget allocation.
**Strategy:**
- Consolidated property data into a unified reporting dashboard.
- Dynamic budget reallocation based on real-time property occupancy and demand signals.
- Cross-property retargeting strategy to capture broader market interest.
**Impact:** Achieved a 35% increase in total sales volume across the portfolio within 6 months.
